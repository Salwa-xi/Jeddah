About Jeddah

Jeddah – the first city in your Arabic map.

Discover Jeddah through the sea, historic Al-Balad, and everyday life on the Red Sea coast.

Red Sea Corniche

Jeddah is a coastal city on the Red Sea. You can see the sea, palm trees, and people walking and greeting each other in Arabic.

Historic Al-Balad

Old houses with wooden balconies, narrow streets, and small markets where you can hear simple Arabic words.

Daily Saudi Life

Families, traditional clothes, coffee, and friendly greetings like “مرحبا” and “أهلاً وسهلاً”.
